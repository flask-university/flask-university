About Flask University

Flask University is an open-source educational initiative that helps developers learn Flask step-by-step by building real-world web applications.

We believe in practical learning, where each level guides you through a focused, hands-on project — from beginner-friendly apps to advanced Flask architecture.

🎯 Our Mission

  • Make Flask accessible to everyone, regardless of background
  • Offer a project-based learning path that grows in complexity
  • Build a collaborative community of learners and mentors

📚 Learning Levels

Each level is a standalone micro-project with clear goals. You can explore our lessons starting from:

  • Level 01 – Hallo Flask

🤝 Contribute
